Chamber 203

In the 1920s and 1930s, the former postal worker Dieter Weise was treated for seven years at the Charite German clinic. Mr. Weise's problem was that he could not control his body in any way. The only thing he could control was speech and breathing. Still, the rest was controlled by a certain Peter, who was a big scum.

The treating doctors could not get to know Peter: he did not enter into contacts with humanity, left all communications to Dieter, and he was completely detached.
